Ichiraku Ramen was founded in 2017 by two Fan of « Naruto ». The Food truck takes the name of famous ramen restaurant from the manga Naruto. This food truck will offer Asian dishes as well as the famous ramen recipe of Teuchi Ichiraku the restaurant cook in "Naruto".

We may not be as talented as Mr. Teuchi Ichiraku, but we will be able to offer you quality dishes.

Our ethics? To cook with fresh products and let everyone eat our dishes.

How can we make sure that everyone can eat them? We will propose dishes according to the customer for example if a customer does not support the gluten and cannot eat pork, we will be able to propose him ramen with something other than pork and noodles made with wheat.
